How to Find a Resident's Program Enrollment

Step 1: Navigate the Dashboard

Begin by selecting 'Dashboard' on the left sidebar to access your main control panel within the Apricot database.

Step 2: Access Participant Profiles

Click on 'Search Records' > 'Participant Profile' to look up individuals enrolled in Housing Hope's programs.

Step 3: Locate Participant

Enter the name of the participant in the search bar to retrieve their profile. Remember, accuracy in spelling is key to finding the correct profile.

Step 4: Review the Participant's Details

Click on any gray text link within the participant's document. This action will direct you to their full profile where you can view comprehensive information about their program involvement.

Accessing a Participant’s Document Folder

After locating the participant’s name, follow the mouse cursor and click on 'View Folder' as indicated to access all documents related to the participant. This is where you'll find detailed enrollment information and other important records.

Guaranteed Accurate Program Enrollment

This visual guide ensures that the 'Program Enrollment' section always contains the correct, up-to-date enrollment status for each resident. By following these steps, you can verify that all program participation details are accurate and reflect the latest information.
