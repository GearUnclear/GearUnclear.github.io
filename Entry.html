Adding Family Members Manually Coming Soon

We are working to iron out a few more kinks before family support coaches can add residents. Stay tuned for updates!

Step 1: Email Christine and Dane

When a newborn joins a family, a child is reunited with a parent, or anyone is added to a lease for any reason, notify the data team promptly. Your notification should include the person's name and birthdate.

Upon receiving this information, the resident will be added to your Apricot property. To complete the necessary data entry for HMIS, you must:

  • Obtain a signed HMIS form by the parent or self if the entrant is an adult (at the time of entry).
  • Complete the Occupancy/Departure Form (available in Apricot under the Household Profile) at the time of entry.
  • Click here for the Occupancy/Departure Guide
  • Complete the Entry Child/Adult Assessment as soon as possible after entry.
  • Submit scanned copies of the birth certificate and social security card to the Participant Profile Folder as soon as possible after entry.

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the entry process is complete!
