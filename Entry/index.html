Adding Family Members

Complete guide for adding new family members to Housing Hope programs in Apricot

1 Email Christine and Dane

When a newborn joins a family, a child is reunited with a parent, or anyone is added to a lease for any reason, notify the data team promptly. Your notification should include the person's name and birthdate.

↓

2 Add to Apricot Property

Upon receiving this information, the resident will be added to your Apricot property.

↓

3 Complete Required Forms

In order to completely enter the necessary data for HMIS you must complete:

  • Signed HMIS form by parent/self if the entrant is an adult (at the time of entry). 📋 Current HMIS Release of Information
  • Occupancy/Departure Form (available in Apricot under the Household Profile) (at the time of entry). 📋 Occupancy/Departure Guide
  • Completed Entry Child/Adult Assessment (at time of entry).
    📋 Adult Entry Assessment 📋 Child Entry Assessment 📋 Fenn Jorstad (Adults Only)
  • Submit scanned birth certificate and social security card to Participant Profile Folder (as soon as possible after entry).
Note: Forms are for reference and guidance. All data must be recorded digitally in Apricot database.
↓

4 Confirm HMIS Data

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the entry process is complete!
