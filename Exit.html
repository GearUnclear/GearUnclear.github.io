Guidelines for Exiting Families Coming Soon

We are working to provide detailed guidelines for exiting families. Stay tuned for updates!

Step 1: Remove from Apricot Property

Upon receiving this information, the family will be removed from your Apricot property.

↓

Step 2: Complete Required Forms

In order to complete the exit process for HMIS you must complete:

  • Signed HMIS form by parent/self if the family member is an adult (at the time of exit).
  • Occupancy/Departure Form (available in Apricot under the Household Profile) (at the time of exit).
    Occupancy/Departure Guide
  • Completed Exit Child/Adult Assessment (at time of exit).
  • Completed Exit from Homeless Housing Datasheet (at time of exit).
    Exit from Homeless Housing Datasheet Guide

When these pieces of data are submitted it alerts the Apricot administrators/data team, to ensure information is rapidly entered into HMIS.

↓

Step 3: Notify the Data Team

When a family is exiting, the data team will be automatically notified.

↓

Step 4: Confirm HMIS Data

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the exit process is complete!
