Adding Family Members to an Existing Family

Complete guide for adding new family members to Housing Hope programs in Apricot

1 Email Christine and Dane

When a newborn joins a family, a child is reunited with a parent, or anyone is added to a lease for any reason, notify the data team promptly. Your notification should include the person's name and birthdate.

This information should be provided to the data team as soon as you gain knowledge of it, and no longer than 3 days after move in.
↓

2 Add to Apricot Property

Upon receiving this information, the resident will be added to your Apricot property.

↓

3 Complete Required Forms

In order to completely enter the necessary data for HMIS you must complete:

Signed HMIS Release of Information
Required by parent/self if the entrant is an adult (at the time of entry).
📋 Current HMIS Release of Information
Occupancy/Departure Form
Available in Apricot (at the time of entry). Found under Household Profile
📋 Occupancy/Departure Guide
Entry Child/Adult Assessment
Completed at time of entry for ALL individuals in the household. Found under Participant Profile
📋 Adult Entry Assessment 📋 Child Entry Assessment 📋 Fenn Jorstad (Adults Only)
Note: Forms are for reference and guidance. All data must be recorded digitally in Apricot database.
Supporting Documents
Submit scanned documents as soon as possible after entry. Found under Participant Profile Folder
• Birth certificate (for children)
• License or State ID (for adults)
• Social security card (all individuals)
↓

4 Confirm HMIS Data

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the entry process is complete!
