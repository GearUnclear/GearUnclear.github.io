Overview & Purpose

Timely reporting and documentation of incidents ensures the safety and well-being of our residents, staff, and community. This protocol outlines the steps, categories, and timelines for submitting incident reports.

Getting Started

Access the Incident Report Form:

Open Incident Report Form

After submission, you have the option to save the form. For photos or additional documentation (e.g., police reports), email them to frontdesk@housinghope.org with the property name and date of occurrence.

Emergency Procedures

If an incident is a true emergency, dial 911 immediately.

  • Life-threatening injuries or illnesses: Call 911 or go to the Emergency Room.
  • Non-life-threatening issues: Visit a Walk-in Clinic.

Incident Categories

  • Personal Injury (staff, resident, client, volunteer, visitor)
  • Property Damage (theft, fire, building damage)
  • Police/Fire/Ambulance Call
  • Public Relations Issues (litigation threatened, media inquiries, etc.)
  • Program/Client Related (rule violations, threats)

Reporting Requirements

Include the following information in every report:

  • Date & time of incident
  • Location/address
  • Description of what happened
  • Who was involved and any witnesses (names, positions/relations)
  • Type & severity of harm or injury
  • Medical treatments administered
  • Steps taken to address the incident
  • Attach any police reports, photos, videos, or documents

Employee Incidents

  • Include all general reporting requirements above
  • Worker's name & contact information
  • Details of duties at time of incident
  • Near-miss conditions or safety violations present
  • Corrective actions taken

Notify department supervisor within 2 hours.

Complete the form within 24 hours.

Submission & Follow-up

  • Email photos/attachments to frontdesk@housinghope.org referencing the incident report.
  • All reports are logged and forwarded to the Strategic Leadership Team and Department Directors.
  • The Department Director will coordinate further action based on safety policies, insurance, training, PR, and fiscal impact.

For questions, contact your Leadership Team or Announcements@housinghope.org
