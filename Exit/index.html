Family Exit Process

Complete guide for exiting families from Housing Hope programs in Apricot

1 Be prepared to exit outgoing residents

When you learn that a resident will be leaving, begin preparing for the required exit interview. The exit procedure is similar for both whole family and individual exits.

Key Point: You only need to complete "Exit" Child/Adult Assessment forms for the family members who are actually leaving.

↓

2 Complete Required Forms

In order to complete the exit process for HMIS you must complete:

Occupancy/Departure Form
Only need to complete one per family. Available in Apricot (at the time of exit). Found under Household Profile
📋 Occupancy/Departure Guide
Exit Child/Adult Assessment
Must be completed for each individual. Available in Apricot for ALL individuals in the household. Found under Participant Profile
📋 Adult Exit Assessment 📋 Child Exit Assessment 📋 Fenn Jorstad (Adults Only)
Note: Forms are for reference and guidance. All data must be recorded digitally in Apricot database.
Exit from Homeless Housing Datasheet
One for the whole family. Available in Apricot (at time of exit). Found under Household Profile
📋 Exit from Homeless Housing Datasheet Guide

When these pieces of data are submitted, it alerts the Apricot administrators/data team, to ensure information is rapidly entered into HMIS.

↓

3 Data Team is Notified

When a family is exited, the data team will be automatically notified, and will follow up with any questions.

↓

4 Confirm HMIS Data

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the exit process is complete!
