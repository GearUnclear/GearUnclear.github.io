Adding Family Members Manually Coming Soon

We are working to iron out a few more kinks before family support coaches can add residents. Stay tuned for updates!

Step 1: Email Christine and Dane

When a newborn joins a family, a child is reunited with a parent, or anyone is added to a lease for any reason, notify the data team promptly. Your notification should include the person's name and birthdate.

↓

Step 2: Add to Apricot Property

Upon receiving this information, the resident will be added to your Apricot property.

↓

Step 3: Complete Required Forms

In order to completely enter the necessary data for HMIS you must complete:

  • Signed HMIS form by parent/self if the entrant is an adult (at the time of entry).
  • Occupancy/Departure Form (available in Apricot under the Household Profile) (at the time of entry).

    • Occupancy/Departure Guide

  • Completed Entry Child/Adult Assessment (as soon as possible after entry).
  • Submit scanned birth certificate and social security card to Participant Profile Folder (as soon as possible after entry).
↓

Step 4: Confirm HMIS Data

After the submitted data is replicated in the HMIS database, the entry process is complete!
